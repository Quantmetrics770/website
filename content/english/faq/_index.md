{{< faq "What types of Modeling & Simulation services does Quantmetrics offer?" >}}
Quantmetrics provides a comprehensive range of pharmacometrics services including Non-Compartmental Analysis (NCA), Population PK/PD modeling, Physiologically-Based Pharmacokinetic (PBPK) modeling, and Clinical Trial Simulation. We support all stages of drug development, from preclinical ADME/PK characterization through Phase III regulatory submissions. We also develop custom software tools and interactive dashboards for data exploration and reporting.
{{</ faq >}}

{{< faq "What therapeutic areas and molecule types do you have experience with?" >}}
Our team has extensive experience across a wide range of therapeutic areas and molecule types, including small molecules and biologics (monoclonal antibodies, peptides, ADCs). We have worked on projects spanning oncology, immunology, infectious diseases, CNS, metabolic disorders, and more. Our expertise covers both standard and complex pharmacokinetic profiles, including target-mediated drug disposition (TMDD) and non-linear PK.
{{</ faq >}}

{{< faq "What software platforms do you use?" >}}
We are proficient in all industry-recognized software platforms, including NONMEM, Monolix, R, PK-Sim/MoBi (Open Systems Pharmacology), Phoenix WinNonlin, and Simcyp. We also develop custom R-Shiny applications for interactive data visualization, simulation, and automated reporting. Our deliverables are designed to meet regulatory standards and CDISC compliance requirements.
{{</ faq >}}

{{< faq "How does a typical engagement work?" >}}
Every project begins with an initial consultation to understand your specific needs and objectives. We then prepare a tailored proposal outlining the scope of work, timeline, and deliverables. Once agreed, our team works closely with yours throughout the project, providing regular updates and interim results. Final deliverables typically include a comprehensive report, model files, and datasets in submission-ready formats.
{{</ faq >}}

{{< faq "At what stage of drug development should I engage a pharmacometrics consultant?" >}}
The earlier the better. Ideally, pharmacometrics should be integrated from the preclinical stage to support translational modeling and First-in-Human dose projections. However, we can add value at any stage — from optimizing Phase I/II study designs and dose selection, to supporting Phase III exposure-response analyses and regulatory submissions. Even post-approval, M&S can inform label extensions and pediatric development programs.
{{</ faq >}}

{{< faq "Can Quantmetrics support regulatory submissions?" >}}
Yes. We prepare submission-ready population PK and exposure-response analyses that meet FDA, EMA, and PMDA expectations. Our reports follow current regulatory guidance documents and include all necessary documentation for IND/NDA/MAA filings. We can also assist with responding to regulatory questions and preparing briefing documents for Health Authority meetings.
{{</ faq >}}

{{< faq "Do you offer training and workshops?" >}}
Absolutely. We offer customized hands-on training sessions and workshops on pharmacometrics methodologies, including Population PK/PD modeling, PBPK modeling with PK-Sim/MoBi, and data analysis with R. Our trainings are designed for preclinical and clinical development teams and combine theoretical foundations with practical case studies from the pharmaceutical industry.
{{</ faq >}}
